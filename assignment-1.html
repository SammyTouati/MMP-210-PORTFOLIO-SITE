Assignment #1: Self-Portrait


Link to Site: C L I C K   H E R E

The goal of this assignment, was to draw a self-portrait of us using p5.js, and then animate our drawing to make it perform some kind of action, or to somewhat enhance movement in some way. So I added this feature of being able to hover and control the position of my glasses, and putting them on my face will help the self-portrait look more....like myself. Also included a little mustache for the fun of it because why not.
